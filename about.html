About StoriesHub

StoriesHub is a simple, fast, and beautiful place to read and share short stories. We believe everyone has a story worth telling.

Built as a lightweight static website, StoriesHub runs on GitHub Pages and needs no servers. You can publish, explore, and enjoy stories on any device.

  • Clean, mobile‑first reading experience
  • Categories & tags for discovery
  • Open, simple, and privacy‑friendly
